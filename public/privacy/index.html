Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Miklus Property Maintenance (“MPM,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you visit mpmnewjersey.com, request an estimate, or submit a lead form on our website or through our advertising on Meta (Facebook/Instagram).

Information we collect

Depending on how you contact us, we may collect:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • City, ZIP code, or service address
  • Project details you choose to share (for example, patio, walkway, or drainage work)
  • Messages you send us by phone, text, or email
  • Basic technical data from your browser (such as device type and pages visited) through standard analytics or advertising tools

How we use your information

We use this information to:

  • Respond to estimate requests and schedule site visits
  • Call or text you about your project
  • Prepare proposals and perform contracted work
  • Improve our website and measure whether our ads are working
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

Lead forms and advertising

If you submit a form on Meta (Facebook/Instagram) or on our site, your responses are delivered to MPM so we can contact you. Meta may also process your data under its own policies when you interact with our ads or forms on its platforms.

Service providers

We may share information with trusted providers who help us operate our business, such as:

  • Website hosting and form delivery services
  • Scheduling and job management tools (for example, Jobber)
  • Meta for advertising and lead delivery
  • Analytics or measurement tools connected to our website or ads

These providers may only use your information to perform services for us, subject to their own privacy terms.

Retention

We keep contact and project information as long as needed to respond to your request, perform work, and maintain business records, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

You may ask us to update or delete your contact information where applicable. You can also adjust ad preferences in your Meta account settings. To stop marketing texts from us, reply STOP to a message we sent you.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, but no website or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version.
